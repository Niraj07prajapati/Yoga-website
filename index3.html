Hatha Yoga

Hatha Yoga is a gentle and foundational practice that focuses on breath control and proper alignment of poses. It is suitable for all levels, including beginners.

Join us for a Hatha Yoga class to cultivate strength, flexibility, and relaxation.

  • Class Duration: 60 minutes
  • Intensity Level: Beginner-Friendly
  • Benefits: Improved flexibility, stress reduction, balance, and mind-body connection.

Vinyasa Yoga

Vinyasa Yoga is a dynamic and fluid yoga practice that synchronizes breath with movement. It is ideal for those seeking a more energetic and challenging class.

Join us for a Vinyasa Flow class to build strength, improve stamina, and find a state of flow.
