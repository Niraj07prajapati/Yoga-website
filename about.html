Our History


Yoga Kendra was founded in 1998 with a vision to create a space where individuals can discover the transformative power of yoga. Over the years, we have nurtured a vibrant community and helped countless students embark on their yoga journey.
The history of yoga classes is intertwined with the ancient practice of yoga itself.

Our Mission


At Yoga Kendra, our mission is to empower individuals to cultivate balance and well-being through the practice of yoga as a means to enhance their physical,mental and spiritual well-being.
We are dedicated to providing a supportive and inclusive environment where students can explore their potential, enhance self-awareness, and foster a deeper connection with themselves.

Our Approach to Yoga Coaching


At Yoga Kendra, we follow a holistic approach to yoga coaching. Our classes focus on integrating breath, movement, and mindfulness to promote physical strength, flexibility, and mental clarity. We emphasize proper alignment and offer modifications to cater to individual needs and abilities. Our experienced instructors provide personalized guidance, creating a supportive environment that encourages self-exploration and growth.
