Know our Instructors

Ram singh

Hatha Yoga Instructor

Hatha yoga instructors focus on the physical practice of yoga, including postures (asanas) and breath control (pranayama). They typically teach a balanced combination of stretching, strengthening, and relaxation techniques.

Devendra kumar yadav

Vinyasa Yoga Instructor

Vinyasa yoga instructors guide students through flowing sequences of yoga postures, synchronized with breath. This style emphasizes continuous movement and smooth transitions between poses.

Ramya kumari

Ashtanga Yoga Instructor

Ashtanga yoga instructors teach a specific style of yoga that follows a structured sequence of postures. Students progress through a set series of poses, gradually advancing as they develop strength, flexibility, and focus.

Amul kumar

Yoga Therapy Instructor

Yoga therapy instructors have additional training in using yoga techniques to address specific health conditions or individual needs. They adapt yoga practices to support the physical, mental, and emotional well-being of their clients.

Chulbuli kumari

Meditation Instructor

Meditation instructors specialize in guiding students through various meditation techniques and practices. They may incorporate mindfulness, visualization, breath awareness, or other meditation methods to cultivate calmness and mental clarity.
